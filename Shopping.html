Shopping in Copenhagen

There are plenty of shopping possibilities here in Copenhagen.
The most popular shopping place in Copenhagen is “Strøget”. It’s a long walking street that starts at Rådhuspladsen (the town hall square) and continues all the way to Kongens Nytorv.
There are all kind of shops, eating places and small sights. If you are a person who likes some of the more expensive shops, you can find Gucci, Louis Vuitton etc in the middle of Strøget.
Another great shopping place is Fisketorvet. It’s a big mall where they have all kind of shops and it’s easy you get to. You just need to take the train to Dybbølsbro station and walk 5 min.
If you like food Torvehallerne is the place to go. They have all kind of food from all around the world. Torvehallerne is located next to Nørreport station.
