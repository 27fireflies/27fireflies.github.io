Rysensteen Gymnasium

Rysensteen is a school situated in inner Copenhagen, founded by Laura Engelhardt and opened in 1881.
Since 2015, we have upheld the principles of NORA:
  • Nysgerrighed
  • Ordentlighed
  • Rummelighed
  • Ansvarlighed
which translates to Curiousity, Orderliness, Inclusiveness and Responsibility.
